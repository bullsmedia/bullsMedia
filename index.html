What We Do
We are an interdisciplinary organization on campus focused on providing students with opportunities in the fields of media and culture, providing facilities and equipment for students to highlight their own talents and creations, along with other on campus resources to serve our USF community! With our state-of-the-art recording studio, equipment, radio services, and events, you're sure to find something that can help you follow your passion.
Our recording studio has state-of-the-art equipment, including instruments, computers, and professional-grade software. Best of all, our entire suite is free to use for all USF students! Bulls Media fosters students' creativity so anyone can make their dreams a reality. Come use our tools and realize your creative potential.
Have some free time? Why not check out our podcast station? Come turn your ideas into reality, and let your voice be heard!
